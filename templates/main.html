Dow Jones Industrial Average

Trading Hours: Open: 9.30 AM EST | Close: 16.00 PM EST

Overview

The Dow Jones Industrial Average, Dow Jones, or simply the Dow, is a stock market index of 30 prominent companies listed on stock exchanges in the United States. The DJIA is one of the oldest and most commonly followed equity indices. The Dow Jones is price-weighted, unlike stock indices which use market capitalization like the S&P 500 or the DAX.

Calculation

In order to calculate the Dow Jones, the sum of all 30 stocks is divided by a divisor (Dow Divisor). The divisor is adjusted in case of stock splits, spinoffs or similar structural changes, to ensure that such events do not in themselves alter the value of the DJIA. Since November 4, 2021 the Dow Divisor is 0.151727525995384, which is means that for every $1 change in price in a stock the Dow Jones moves 6.590761918 points.
